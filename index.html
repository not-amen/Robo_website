Frequently Asked Questions

How old is our Solar System? +
Our Solar System formed about 4.6 billion years ago from a giant rotating cloud of gas and dust called the solar nebula.
What is the largest planet? +
Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System, with a mass more than twice that of all other planets combined.
How far is the Sun from Earth? +
The average distance from Earth to the Sun is about 93 million miles (150 million kilometers), also known as 1 Astronomical Unit (AU).
Can humans live on other planets? +
Currently, no other planet in our Solar System can naturally support human life. However, Mars is being studied as a potential candidate for future human colonization with the help of technology.
